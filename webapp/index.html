Our application is not just another application about global warming. It is designed to raise awareness about our environment (check out Facts section) but most of all, it can help you to step up and TAKE action. We offer easy ideas on how to do that in our Tips section. World map section offers interaction by giving you the possibility to select a country and we will display the carbon footprint, global warming and sea level in a chart.
Remember: Nature, to be commanded, must be obeyed.

The name idea came in our search for originality. "Bodhi" is the sanscrit meaning of "knowledge" and "enlightenment". So, "Eco Bodhi" application was originally planned to raise a flag about our environmental issues, how each one of us contributes to it ..but most of all, how we can make a difference in creating a better world. Most of the information and images used in the app are not part of our creation, due to the scope of the project and the timeframe.
